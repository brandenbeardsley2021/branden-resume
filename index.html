01

Adyen's Uplift product — like Stripe's Authorization Boost — only lands if the PM can design rigorous A/B tests against competing processors and turn the results into commercial decisions. This is the current day-to-day at Adyen.

  • No structured methodology to compare processors
  • Account teams couldn't run tests without specialist support
  • Competitors made unverifiable authorization claims
  • Product adoption stalled without third-party proof points
How it was solved
  • Designed controlled A/B test methodology for head-to-head processor comparison

↔ Stripe Radar · Dispute Management
